Sensation Amsterdam

Sensation is an indoor electronic dance music event which originated in the Netherlands diving into Black and white. Sensation White used to be a trance event, but in later years it has become a pure house event. All the attendees are required to wear white and the Amsterdam ArenA itself is decorated to match. The Amsterdam ArenA event takes place every year on the first Saturday in July.
